The tidy dataset lists the means and the standard deviations of the accelerometer and gyroscope signal measurements. Means are available for measurements that
are collected for X, Y and Z axis. 

The mean is calculated for the following features for time and frequency domain signals:
A) Time domain measurements:
---------------------------
Body Accelaration
Body Gravity Accelaration
Body Accelaration Jerk
Body Angular Velocity
Body Angular Velocity Jerk
Body Accelaration Magnitude
Body Angular Velocity Magnitude
Body Angular Velocity Jerk Magnitude 

B) Frequency Domain measurements:
--------------------------------
Body Accelaration - frequency domain measurements
Body Accelaration Frequency  
Body Accelaration Jerk  
Body Accelaration Jerk Frequency  
Body Angular Velocity  
Body Angular Velocity Frequency  
Body Accelaration Magnitude  
Body Accelaration Magnitude Frquency  
Body Body Accelaration Jerk  
Body Body Accelaration Jerk Frequency  
Body Body Angular Velocity Magnitude  
Body Body Angular Velocity Magnitude Frequency  
Body Body Angular Velocity Jerk Magnitude  
Body Body Angular Velocity Jerk Magnitude Frequency  

The standard deviation is calculated for the following features for time and frequency domain signals:
A) Time domain measurements:
---------------------------
Body Accelaration
Body Gravity Acc
Body Gravity Acc Jerk
Body Angular Velocity
Body Angular Velocity Jerk
Body Accelaration Magnitude
Body Gravity Acc Magnitude
Body Accelaration Jerk Magnitude
Body Angular Velocity Magnitude
Body Angular Velocity Jerk Magnitude

B) Frequency Domain measurements:
--------------------------------
Body Accelaration Frequency
Body Accelaration Jerk Frequency
Body Angular Velocity Frequency
Body Accelaration Frequency Magnitude
Body Body Accelaration Jerk Magnitude
Body Body Angular Velocity Magnitude
Body Body Angular Velocity Jerk Magnitude
